About Me

Hi, I'm Fahad, a graphic designer and tech content creator with a strong focus on display technology and consumer hardware. I specialize in creating visual designs for YouTube channels and producing informative tech content that helps people understand complex technology in a simple way.

My work involves researching and analyzing display manufacturing brands, monitor technologies, and panel types such as IPS, VA, TN, and OLED. I create detailed reviews and buying guides that explain performance, image quality, and real-world usability so viewers can make better decisions before purchasing tech products.

Alongside design, I handle video editing and content production for YouTube and Facebook, where I present tech analysis, product insights, and comparisons in a clear and engaging format. I regularly communicate with brands and manufacturers to gather technical information and provide accurate, research-based reviews.

I'm passionate about technology, visual storytelling, and educating consumers through digital media. My goal is to combine design, research, and technical knowledge to create content that is both visually engaging and genuinely useful for the tech community.
